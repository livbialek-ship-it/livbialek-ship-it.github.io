Internships & Roles

I'm a Communications major with a SLAM minor at Tulane University (Class of 2028). My academic and professional work sits at the intersection of marketing, media, and entrepreneurship.

I founded my first clothing line, LIVincomfort, in 2020 — it was picked up by Royal NY boutique. More recently I co-founded Campus Closet, a peer-to-peer college clothing rental and resale platform, which I pitched at Tulane's Startup Lab Demo Day.

Outside the classroom I've interned with Tulane Athletics, N Network, and Taste & Talk — all focused on marketing strategy, content creation, and social media. I'm also passionate about community leadership through Sigma Delta Tau and Relay for Life.
